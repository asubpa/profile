About Me

I am a computer vision researcher graduated from Tokyo Institute of Technology. I work as a researcher at the National Institute of Informatics, focusing on image acquisition systems, physics-based computer vision, and spectral reconstruction.

  • Learning-based imaging methods
  • Novel sensor & unconventional imaging
